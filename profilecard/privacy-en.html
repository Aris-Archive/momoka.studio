Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 28, 2026

My Profile Card (the "App") is provided by Momoka (the "Developer") as an individual developer. This policy explains what information is collected while using the App and how it is handled.

1. Information We Collect

The App handles information through the following two channels.

  • Information you provide directly: Any data you enter or choose in the App — such as your selected SNS platform, handle or URL, card name, and the photo used as a card background along with its position, scale, and tint adjustments — is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to the Developer's servers.
  • Information collected through advertising services: The App displays ads using Google AdMob. AdMob may collect advertising identifiers, device information, and IP address.

2. Storage and Retention

The SNS card information and background photos you create are stored only in local storage on your device. They are not automatically synced to any external server or cloud. The backup feature lets you export your data as a file; where that file is saved or shared is determined entirely by you through the iOS share sheet. You can also restore your data at any time by importing a backup file.

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party service. Please also review its privacy policy.

  • Google AdMob Privacy Policy

4. Sharing Information with Third Parties

The Developer does not sell or share collected user data with third parties. Information collected through AdMob is handled in accordance with Google's policies.

5. Children's Privacy

This App is not directed at children under the age of 13, and the Developer does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect changes in law or in the App. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date on this page.
